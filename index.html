Outpatient Care Network | United States and United Kingdom

Outpatient care designed to be clear, timely, and easier to access.

Founded in Austin in 2011, HealthCore supports patients across 12 clinics in Texas, Florida, Georgia, London, and Manchester. Our network provides primary care, specialist consultations, chronic disease management, and preventive health programmes with a focus on coordinated outpatient care.
