About Me

A Caribbean soul with Merengue passion

Rhythm in his veins.

Rafa Ramirez is a Merengue maestro from the Dominican Republic, blending traditional Caribbean rhythms with modern flair. His music tells stories of island life, love, and celebration.

With a voice that echoes the waves and beats that move the crowd, Rafa brings the essence of the Caribbean to every performance.

Career

Rising star in Merengue music

From local Dominican festivals to international tours, Rafa has built a career celebrating Merengue's vibrant heritage while pushing its boundaries.
